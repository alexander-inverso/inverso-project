Inverso.

I'm Alexander DTT, and I'm 18. My actual goal in life is to simulate the human body at the molecular level. To develop a digital twin driven by heterogeneous computing: quantum processors for individual interactions, HPC for the broader state, probabilistic and neuromorphic systems for speed. A machine that computes what a drug should be, or how aging works mechanistically, and then how to stop it.

I named my project Inverso because understanding nature requires reverse-engineering it and because revolutions happen when the state of the art is turned upside down.
